Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is a famous South Indian dish made with rice batter and a spicy potato filling. It is crispy on the outside and soft inside, served hot with chutneys and sambar.

Ingredients:

  • Rice batter
  • Potatoes
  • Spices:
    • Mustard seeds
    • Curry leaves
    • Green chilies
  • Oil
  • Salt

Cooking Steps:

  1. Prepare the rice batter and let it ferment.
  2. Cook the potato filling with spices.
  3. Heat the pan and spread the batter thinly.
  4. Add the potato filling in the center.
  5. Fold and serve hot with chutney.

Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a creamy North Indian curry made with paneer, butter, tomatoes, and aromatic spices. It is one of the most popular dishes in Indian restaurants.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer cubes
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Spices:
    • Garam masala
    • Chili powder
    • Coriander powder
  • Fresh cream
  • Salt

Cooking Steps:

  1. Fry paneer cubes lightly in butter.
  2. Prepare the tomato gravy by pureeing and cooking tomatoes.
  3. Add butter and spices to the gravy.
  4. Mix in the paneer cubes.
  5. Garnish with fresh cream and coriander leaves.

Cooking Terms:

Simmer
Cooking slowly on low heat.
Puree
Smooth paste made by blending ingredients.

Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a classic Indian sweet made with milk solids (khoya), fried until golden, and soaked in sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose water. It is a festive favorite.

Ingredients:

  • Khoya (milk solids)
  • Maida (flour)
  • Baking soda
  • Sugar syrup:
    • Sugar
    • Water
    • Cardamom
    • Rose water
  • Ghee or oil for frying

Cooking Steps:

  1. Knead khoya, maida, and soda into a soft dough.
  2. Roll the dough into small balls.
  3. Deep fry on low heat until golden brown.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup with sugar, water, cardamom, and rose water.
  5. Soak the fried balls in syrup and serve warm.

Cooking Terms:

Knead
Working the dough with hands to make it smooth.
Syrup
A thick sweet liquid made from sugar and water.
